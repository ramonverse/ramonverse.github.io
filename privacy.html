Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and handle your personal data when you use our mobile application ("the App"). We understand the importance of privacy and are committed to protecting your information. We only collect the minimum required data necessary to provide you with our services. This Privacy Policy is designed to meet the requirements of the Apple App Store.

1. Information We Collect

We only collect the following types of information that are necessary for the functioning of the App:

  1. Device Information: We may collect certain information about your device, including device model, operating system version, unique device identifier (UDID), and mobile network information. This information is required to ensure compatibility and deliver the best user experience.
  2. Usage Information: We may collect information about how you use the App, such as the features you use, the buttons you click, and the pages you visit. This data helps us understand your preferences and improve the App's performance and functionality.
  3. Crash Reports: In the event of a crash or error while using the App, we may collect crash reports that contain certain technical information related to the incident. This data helps us diagnose and fix issues to enhance the stability of the App.

2. Use of Information

We use the collected information solely for the following purposes:

  1. App Functionality: The device information we collect is used to ensure that the App functions properly on your device and to provide you with the best user experience.
  2. App Improvement: The usage information helps us analyze user behavior, identify trends, and make enhancements to the App's features and performance.
  3. Error Analysis: Crash reports are utilized to investigate and resolve any crashes or errors within the App, ensuring a smooth user experience.

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not share or disclose your personal data to third parties unless required by law or to comply with legal obligations. We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any external entities.

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal data from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. We use industry-standard encryption protocols and follow best practices to safeguard your information.

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, unless a longer retention period is required by law.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware that we have unintentionally collected personal information from a child under 13, we will promptly delete it from our records.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to modify or update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be effective immediately upon posting the updated Privacy Policy within the App. We recommend that you review this Privacy Policy periodically.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us at ramonverse@proton.me.

By using the App, you signify your acceptance of this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with this policy, please refrain from using the App.
